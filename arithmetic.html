Arithmetic

Why is This Important?

Arithmetic is the foundation of all math. It includes working with numbers using the operations of addition, subtraction, multiplication and division. Understanding arithmetic will help boost your problem-solving skills. It is a crucial life skill, and it can be a lot of fun! All the other math you will encounter in school is built on arithmetic.
