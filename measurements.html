Why is This Important?

When we go to the doctors office, the nurse and the doctor take our weight in pounds and measure our height in feet and inches. They take our temperature in degrees Fahrenheit, and they might even record the measurements of our pulse and our blood pressure. But measurements happen in all sorts of places, not just doctors offices. Measurements make our modern life possible. We use measurements for cooking, for time management, and for navigation and travel.
