Why is This Important?

Math is the key to success. Its a way for you to understand the world. It helps us make sense of things happening around us.

In the morning, you might see a weather report that tells you what to expect from the day. In the afternoon, you might go shopping for groceries. In the evening, you might help someone bake a cake for someone's birthday.

Every step of the way, math is there to help you accomplish these tasks.

Mathematics is a life skill that will help you understand counting and arithmetic as well as show you how to manage money and time. It's there to help you plan your day and your future.

What do you want to be when you grow up? Did you know that math is a skill that leads to great jobs? Ask a trusted adult how math might be a part of the job you dream of.
